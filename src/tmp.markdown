## Monday, March 4, 2024

### Lunch: Pumpkin Spice Pancakes

**Ingredients**
- 200 grams all-purpose flour
- 4 grams baking powder
- 5 grams pumpkin spice mix
- 2 grams salt
- 260 milliliters milk
- 60 grams raw pumpkin puree
- 50 grams white sugar
- 2 large eggs
- Nutmeg whipped cream
- Candied pecans
- Applewood smoked bacon

**Steps**
1. Combine the flour, baking powder, pumpkin spice mix, and salt in a large bowl.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, pumpkin puree, sugar, and eggs.
3. Gradually mix the wet mixture into the dry mixture until well combined.
4. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
5. Pour a quarter of the batter onto the skillet.
6. Flip the pancake once the bubbles pop and don't fill back in.
7. Cook the pancake until it is golden brown on both sides.
8. Repeat steps 5-7 with the remaining batter.
9. Serve the pancakes with nutmeg whipped cream and candied pecans on top and a side of applewood smoked bacon.


### Dinner: Grilled Flank Steak

**Ingredients**
- 800 grams flank steak
- Herb chimichurri
- Roasted baby potatoes
- Grilled asparagus
- Corn on the cob
- Tomato avocado salad

**Steps**
1. Season the steak with salt and pepper to taste and brush it with the herb chimichurri.
2. Preheat a grill to high heat.
3. Grill the steak on each side for 5-7 minutes until it reaches a medium-rare doneness.
4. Set the steak aside to rest.
5. Serve the steak with the roasted baby potatoes, grilled asparagus, corn on the cob, and tomato avocado salad.

### Dinner: Taco Tuesday

**Ingredients**
- Ground beef tacos
- Mexican rice
- Refried beans
- Corn and avocado salad

**Steps**
1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until it is well-browned.
2. Drain the beef and season it with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Warm the taco shells in a 350°F oven for 5-7 minutes until crisp.
4. Fill the taco shells with the ground beef and your choice of toppings.
5. Serve the tacos with the Mexican rice, refried beans, and corn and avocado salad.
